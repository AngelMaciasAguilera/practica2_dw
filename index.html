MY BIO

From the Streets of New Orleans

It all began in the birthplace of jazz—New Orleans.
Growing up in this legendary city, I was immersed in the
vibrant rhythms and soulful sounds that filled the
streets. The energy of live music was everywhere, from
brass bands parading through the French Quarter to la-
te-night jam sessions in hidden clubs. As a young musician,
I was captivated by the saxophone’s ability to express raw
emotion, and I knew early on that jazz would be my path.

My first performances were in small, smoky bars where
the audience felt every note and improvisation was king.
The freedom of New Orleans jazz allowed me to experi-
ment, to push boundaries, and to find my voice as a
solo artist. This city not only gave me a stage but also
taught me the importance of storytelling through music,
where each performance becomes a journey of its own.
Today, that spirit of New Orleans lives on in everything I
play, from intimate solos to full-blown concerts, keeping
the heartbeat of jazz alive and evolving.

Inspired by the Greats

From the earliest days of my musical journey, legends like John
Coltrane, Miles Davis, Charlie Parker, and Frank Sinatra have been
my guiding lights. Their mastery of their instruments, or in Sina-
tra’s case, his voice, taught me that music is more than just no-
tes—it's about storytelling and connection. Coltrane’s spiritual
depth, Parker’s innovative bebop phrasing, and Sinatra’s unmat
ched ability to breathe life into lyrics deeply influenced how I
approach the saxophone. I’ve studied their recordings for hours,
not to mimic them, but to understand the emotional power
behind every note, every breath.

What inspired me most about these artists was their fearless
approach to music, their constant push beyond boundaries. They
refused to be confined by tradition, creating something entirely
new while respecting the past. That same spirit fuels my playing
today. Whether improvising on stage or composing in the studio, I
strive to channel their innovation and passion, crafting my own
voice while honoring the rich legacy of jazz that they helped shape.

A Global Stage

As my music has grown, so too has the reach of my perfor-
mances, taking me far beyond the streets of New Orleans.
Jazz has always been a universal language, and I’ve had
the privilege of sharing it with audiences around
EE.UU—from intimate clubs in New York and bustling jazz
festivals in Virginia, to collaborations with international mu-
sicians that have enriched my sound. Each new place I
visit and each stage I perform on adds a new layer to my
understanding of jazz, bringing different cultural influences
into my music.

What I’ve learned on this global journey is that while jazz
has deep roots in a specific time and place, it speaks to
people from all walks of life, transcending borders and
boundaries. It’s an honor to see my music resonate with
diverse audiences, each of whom brings their own interpre-
tations and emotions to the experience. This connection
across cultures is one of the most rewarding aspects of
being an artist, and it continues to fuel my passion for ex-
ploring the endless possibilities of jazz.

The Journey Continues

Today, my musical journey continues to evolve, taking the founda-
tion of New Orleans jazz and weaving it into new, contemporary
sounds. As a solo saxophonist, I’ve embraced the freedom of blen-
ding tradition with innovation, creating a unique style that resona-
tes with both jazz purists and new listeners alike. Each performan-
ce is a reflection of this evolution—an invitation to experience the ti-
meless spirit of jazz through fresh interpretations and soulful im-
provisation.

Whether on stage at a major jazz festival or in the intimate atmos-
phere of a small venue, I strive to connect deeply with my audience.
The saxophone becomes an extension of my voice, telling stories
that words cannot capture. It’s in these moments that I feel the
legacy of jazz flow through me, combining the past with the pre-
sent, and breathing new life into every note. The improvisational
nature of jazz allows me to explore uncharted territory, ensuring
that no two performances are ever the same.

As I look ahead, I’m excited to continue pushing boundaries and
sharing my passion for jazz with audiences around the world. My
journey is far from over, and every concert, every collaboration, and
every note played adds to the ever-growing tapestry of my musical
expression. I invite you to join me on this path, where the rhythm of
the past meets the innovation of the future. Together, we’ll keep
the spirit of jazz alive and thriving in the modern age.
