MY BIO

From the Streets of New Orleans

Born and raised in the heart of jazz—New Orleans—I was shaped by its vibrant music scene. From street brass bands to late-night jams, I found my voice as a saxophonist in smoky bars, where improvisation and raw emotion reigned. Today, I carry the spirit of New Orleans in every note, keeping the essence of jazz alive in my music.

Inspired by the Greats

From the start, legends like Coltrane, Davis, Parker, and Sinatra shaped my musical path. Their mastery taught me that music is storytelling and connection. I’ve studied their work to grasp the emotion behind every note, not to copy, but to innovate. Their fearless push beyond boundaries inspires me to honor jazz’s legacy while forging my own voice.

A Global Stage

As my music has evolved, so has my reach, taking me beyond New Orleans to stages across the U.S. and beyond. Jazz is a universal language, and performing in places like New York and Virginia, alongside international collaborations, has enriched my sound. I've learned that while jazz has roots, it connects with people everywhere, transcending borders and cultures, fueling my passion to keep exploring its endless possibilities.

The Journey Continues

My musical journey blends the roots of New Orleans jazz with contemporary sounds, creating a unique style that connects with both jazz purists and new listeners. Each performance reflects this evolution—bringing fresh interpretations and soulful improvisation. Whether at festivals or intimate venues, I strive to tell stories through the saxophone, keeping jazz’s legacy alive by mixing tradition with innovation. As I continue to push boundaries, I invite you to join me in celebrating the timeless spirit of jazz.
